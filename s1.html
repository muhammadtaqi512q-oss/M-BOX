🤖 AI Command Tips

📝 How it works

Type commands in the AI Block (inside the builder), one per line. Each command starts with a keyword followed by : or ; and the value. The AI reads your commands and adds matching blocks.

🔤 Case‑insensitive & flexible separators

Keywords work in uppercase, lowercase, or mixed case. Use : or ; as separator.

title: MT king
TITLE; MT king
text: I am very sad

🅰️ Heading / Title

title: Welcome
heading: My Site
hero: I am very sad

📃 Text / Paragraph

text: Hello world
paragraph: This is my story

🔘 Button (name + link)

button; name; Hello World; link; deepseek
button: Click Me | https://google.com
Shortcuts: deepseek, chatgpt, google, youtube, github, etc.

🎨 Background

background: image | https://example.com/photo.jpg
background: navy
background: mint
background: color | #ff5500

⚡ Action commands

download     → exports HTML file
preview      → opens live preview popup
live url     → generates shareable link

🪄 Multiple commands on one line

title; MT king text; mt king
button; name; abc; link; chatgpt download

🚀 Full example

title: I am very sad
button; name; Hello World; link; deepseek
background: image
download
